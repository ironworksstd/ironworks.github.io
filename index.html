About Fall Ball

Fall Ball is a short-session 2D arcade game built for touch. Players control a bouncing ball through falling obstacles and try to rack up high scores. Designed with accessibility and replayability in mind.

  • Simple one-touch controls
  • Progressive difficulty and daily challenges
  • Minimalist pixel art and crisp audio
